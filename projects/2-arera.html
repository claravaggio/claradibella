Scenario
In 2050, Exhoil, once a major resource extractor, faces the reality of soil death caused by urbanization, deforestation, and unsustainable farming. The soil, no longer able to absorb carbon or support life, is declared dead, marking the collapse of vital ecosystems. In response, Exhoil shifts its focus from taking resources to giving back.
