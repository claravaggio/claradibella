Mario Dagrada
Editorial design
2021
Brief
Create a monograph on an important figure in the world of graphic design. Decide the topics of internal articles, manage the layout independently as art director. Start by studying the rules of layout and the magazine "Multiverso".
Concept
The designer assigned to my group was Mario Dagrada. The monograph "Mario Dagrada - Tra editoria e pubblicità" aims to tell the story of the designer in its entirety, from his life to his great achievements in the world of communication. Dagrada's work has always alternated between publishing and advertising, making him iconic and recognized thanks to the covers he created for Rizzoli. We started from this very project of his to create all the covers of the internal sections of the monograph and for the cover itself, which takes up the "tear" aesthetic typical of the Rizzoli books made by the designer. In our case we wanted to materially realize the tear on the monograph's dust jacket, to make the artifact more interesting and three-dimensional.
Monograph
In addition to the various sections, each edited by a member of the group, we produced an insert together entitled "the void." The theme of emptiness in our case has been interpreted with the lack of knowledge towards Mario Dagrada's works and projects: in fact, within the sixth rubric you can find graphic illustrations and textual content that tell how to the people the designer's name appears unknown. It all started from a field research regarding the knowledge of Mario Dagrada and it aims to keep alive the memory of the works of this great designer.
