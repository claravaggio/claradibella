Brief
Create the visual identity of the exhibition at Triennale di Milano of speculative design thesis projects from the Final Sinthesis Studio C1 course in Communication Design at Politecnico di Milano. Then build a summary website that showcases all the projects and maintains the overall identity of the exhibition.
Concept
The name of the project is "Anthropogenic narratives: non-communicating human experiences," which perfectly sums up the concept of the course and the exhibition: narrating from a non-anthropocentric point of view, putting nature at the center and showing through natural elements the consequences of climate change. The identity is based on the effect of reaction diffusion, which is applied not only to the title of the exhibition but also to the various projects within the dedicated pages. The website we created is therefore not just a showcase, it embodies the concept of "looking through" to communicate from a new perspective allowing nature itself to become the main character and tell its own story.
