Pinocchio

Videogame development
2022
Brief
Making a video game from the reimagination of a famous fairy tale. Build the game dynamics, program it, make the illustrations and sprites for the backgrounds and characters, tell the new storyline through 3 three-dimensional modeling boards.
Concept
The story of Pinocchio has been reworked to make it a shooter video game. In this version of the story Mangiafuoco kills Geppetto, enraging Pinocchio who craves revenge. In the videogame, the user as Pinocchio must traverse 3 levels: the village, Toyland, and finally Mangiafuoco's circus with the final boss.
Pinocchio
The game is a typical platforming video game, where the user has to shoot at 3 different enemies (the Mangiafuoco's puppets) to defend himself. Life and ammo can be recharged by collecting objects along the way. At each level, progress is saved. If you win, a score is given at the end of the game, calculated by the number of times you have died and the enemies you have killed: this stimulates replayability of the video game to try to beat your personal best. The video game was made with Phaser, an open source framework that allows, using HTML5, Javascript or TypeScript, to develop complete games.
